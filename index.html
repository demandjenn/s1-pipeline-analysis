Key Insights: Events Performance
  • Events drove 147 S1s (20% of all marketing influenced), but declined 91% from Q1 (86) to Q4 (8)
  • ENT segment dominated Events with 85 S1s (58% of all event S1s), but dropped from 54 in Q1 to just 6 in Q4
  • Gladly Events were the top performer generating 90 S1s, followed by Event Demo (38) and External Events (14)
  • Sales Referred Events contributed 48 additional S1s when campaign names contained "event"
  • Critical concern: Events as an ENT pipeline driver have nearly collapsed - needs strategic review
